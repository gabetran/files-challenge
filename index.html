Pedobear

Pedobear is a cartoon brown bear that is often used on online forums as a meme to mock pedophiles

Pedobear is not from a story, although the art style does make it seem like it could've came from the show Adventure Time. Pedobear actually originated from popular Japanese bulletin board website called 2channel. It was introduced as Kuma (Japanese for bear), had no sexual connotations, and appeared as a single lined ASCII art. It eventually evolved into the image above and adopted a new meaning.
